Welcome To Snow View.

Nestled in the heart of Sonamarg, Sonamarg Snow View offers a cozy and memorable stay surrounded by majestic snow-covered mountains and serene landscapes. Whether you're here for a quiet retreat or a scenic adventure, our hotel provides a peaceful atmosphere, warm hospitality, and comfortable rooms to make your visit truly enjoyable. Located close to major attractions, we’re the perfect base to explore the natural beauty of Kashmir. Our team is dedicated to making your stay smooth and relaxing — with simplicity, comfort, and care. Come experience the charm of Sonamarg with us.

  • On-site food and beverage service for guests.
  • In-room dining and delivery of food or amenities.
  • High-speed internet access throughout the hotel.
  • Cleaning and replenishment of toiletries.
  • Entertainment with Netflix or Amazon Prime.
